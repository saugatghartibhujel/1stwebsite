All about me

My name is Saugat Gharti Bhujel. I am 19 years old and currently studying in Grade XII at Southwestern State College, specializing in Computer Science. I consider myself a hardworking, curious, and growth-oriented person who enjoys learning new skills, taking responsibilities, and contributing positively to any team I become a part of.
